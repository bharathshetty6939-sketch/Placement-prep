## 🕒 Speed, Time, and Distance
**Problem:** Train passing a pole/bridge.
- **The Logic:** $Speed$ = $Distance$ / $Time$
- **The Shortcut:** - To convert **m/s to km/hr**: Multiply by $18/5$
  - To convert **km/hr to m/s**: Multiply by $5/18$

## 📈 Percentages & Profit
**Problem:** Finding "Original Value" when a percentage is lost/sold.
- **The Logic:** If $X\%$ is sold, then $(100 - X)\%$ is what remains.
- **Formula:** Total =(remaing value/x)*100.
